Welcome to The Voice: A Tribute to Whitney Houston

Whitney Houston, one of the greatest voices in music history, touched hearts across the globe with her unforgettable talent.

This website is a celebration of her life, music, and legacy—a place where fans can come together to honor the legend. "I decided long ago never to walk in anyone’s shadow.” — Whitney Housto

About Whitney

Whitney Elizabeth Houston was born on August 9, 1963, in Newark, New Jersey. Raised in a musical family, she was destined for greatness. Whitney’s powerful voice, a combination of soul, pop, and gospel influences, catapulted her to international stardom. From her debut album in 1985 to her legendary rendition of "I Will Always Love You" she became a global icon.

Over the course of her career, she won numerous Grammy Awards, American Music Awards, and sold millions of albums worldwide. Beyond her music, Whitney also made her mark in the film industry with her unforgettable role in The Bodyguard (1992), cementing her status as an all-around entertainer.

Whitney Houston’s music continues to inspire generations, and her voice will forever be remembered as one of the greatest in history.
